Parents' testimonials

It is my pleasure to recommend Happy Kids NY Nursery, a Spanish immersion Montessori program. My daughter, Audrey, has attended Happy Kids NY since she was four months old. Audrey recently turned two and during her time at Happy Kids NY we have watched her flourish and grow from an infant to a happy, curious, confident and loving toddler. I attribute much of her growth to her time at Amiguitos.
Gloria Santos is the lead teacher and manager of Happy Kids NY. Gloria is trained in Montessori methods and has created a structured, organized and dynamic classroom environment. Gloria communicates well and often about Audrey’s day-to-day experiences at Happy Kids NY, which is especially important to me as a working parent.
Under the care of Gloria and the other teachers at Happy Kids NY, Audrey has developed language and numeracy skills in both English and Spanish, mastered classroom routines and established warm and loving friendships with her classmates – lovingly referred to as her “amiguitos”.
Over the summer, I had a second child. During this transitional time, Gloria was especially attentive with Audrey and took extra time to make sure she was getting the attention she needed while Mom and Dad attended to the new baby. Gloria sent me extra text messages and pictures so I knew Audrey was doing well in my absence. It is these types of personalized gestures that make Happy Kids NY Nursery so very special.
It is without reservation that I recommend Happy Kids NY to prospective parents.

Deirdre Smith
