Welcome to Happy Kids NY!

Happy Kids NY introduces children to the joy of learning at a young age in a loving, safe, and family-like environment.

Our montessori program's philosophy is influenced by the methodology developed by Dr. Maria Montessori, who believed that if you allow a child to explore and attempt tasks on his or her own, the result is a confident, independent, and self-disciplined child.
